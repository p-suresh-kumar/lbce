About College

Woman is an embodiment of great human virtues and she is the greatest human resource of any society. Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering (for women) encourages and promotes women education and keeps its main focus on women empowerment and their personality development with which they can face the challenges of modern world. The broad objective of the institution is to provide the right opportunities to girl students for learning the higher skills in new technologies and building their extraordinary careers in industry and research organizations.

Acclaimed by the student community as an outstanding women engineering college in the smart city Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering (for women), was established by The Society for Collegiate Education in 2010 with the objective to produce women technologists and scientists who would engage in research, design and development to help building the nation. Our Campus is ideally located in the heart of the city on 10 acre land.

The college is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and it offers high standard technical training. The college offershighly preferred engineering study programs namely :


COURSES

CSEComputer Science Engineering
ECEElectronics And Communications Engineering
EEEElectrical & Electronics Engineering
CIVILCivil Engineering
BSBasic Science

The curriculum places emphasis on basic topics in engineering sciences, design and experimental methods. The College’s faculty and staff strive hard with their limitless potential and technical expertise to create ingenious Engineers.

It has a clear vision of imparting value based and high standard technical education to women and to enable them face the global competition and knowledge challenges of 21st century. The campus life is a perfect learning ambience to aspiring students with facilities such as well-staked modern e-library, large seminar halls, fully furnished laboratories, up to date language laboratories, spacious class rooms, multi-sport play Ground, health centre and cafeteria. The Wi-Fi & Internet includes continuous and uninterrupted internet connectivity to students and faculty members. The Internal Quality Assurance Cell constantly monitors the academic progress of students and the teaching efficiency of the teachers.

Proper care is taken to develop student’s overall personality, bringing out the students hidden virtues through activities such as NCC, NSS, Sports, Nature Club, literary and cultural competitions. The College enjoys outstanding success in inter-university sports competitions producing many top class sports personalities.

Safety & Security of girl students is the primary concern of the college. It is a highly Secured and Sophisticated Campus which provides UV treated safe drinking water for entire campus and well experienced security personnel positioned at all the gates. The entire college campus is kept under the surveillance of CCTV Camera for making the college a ragging free campus.
